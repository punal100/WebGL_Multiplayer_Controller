# Brand Logo

Drop your own logo here to replace the default placeholder.

## How to change the logo

Just replace one of the files below with your own image (keep the same name
and extension). The app auto-detects whichever one exists — no code changes
needed.

Supported files (in priority order):

- `logo.png`
- `logo.jpg`
- `logo.jpeg`
- `logo.webp`
- `logo.svg`

## Ratio

Your logo can be **any ratio between 1:6 and 6:1** (very tall to very wide).
The layout adapts automatically and will never obstruct the player controls.

## Placement

- **Main page**: logo sits in the top-left corner.
- **Controller page**: logo sits in the bottom-center, clear of the controls.

> Note: files in this `public/logo` folder are served as-is. After swapping
> the file, refresh the page (hard-refresh may be needed to bypass cache).
